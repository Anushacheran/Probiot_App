ABOUT PROBIOT

ProBiot is a lightweight and interactive web application that brings essential protein sequence analysis to your fingertips. Whether you are a student exploring bioinformatics or a researcher needing quick insights, ProBiot makes sequence analysis simple, fast, and accessible from any browser.

Instead of running heavy tools or complex scripts, you can now calculate key biochemical properties with just a few clicks. ProBiot was designed with clarity, usability, and efficiency in mind, making it an excellent companion for both learning and research.
